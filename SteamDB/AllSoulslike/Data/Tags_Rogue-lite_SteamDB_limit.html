The 'Rogue-lite' tag on SteamDB lists games that offer a roguelike experience, which is characterized by procedurally generated content, permanent death, and incremental upgrades. In these games, players embark on a challenging journey through procedurally generated levels or worlds, where every attempt may result in death but also brings experience and progress. This tag encompasses a diverse range of genres, from action to strategy and RPG, each with unique mechanics that add depth and replayability to the roguelike formula.
